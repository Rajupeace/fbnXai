**About Vignan University (VFSTR):**
Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology & Research (VFSTR) is a Deemed to be University located in Vadlamudi, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. It provides high-quality education in Engineering, Management, and Applied Sciences.

**Grading System:**
The university follows a Choice Based Credit System (CBCS).
- **O (Outstanding)**: 10 Grade Points (Marks: 90-100)
- **S (Excellent)**: 9 Grade Points (Marks: 80-89)
- **A (Very Good)**: 8 Grade Points (Marks: 70-79)
- **B (Good)**: 7 Grade Points (Marks: 60-69)
- **C (Fair)**: 6 Grade Points (Marks: 50-59)
- **D (Pass)**: 5 Grade Points (Marks: 40-49)
- **F (Fail)**: <40 Marks

**Library Facilities:**
The NTR Vignan Library is the central library.
- **Working Hours**: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM (Mon-Sat).
- **Digital Library**: Access to IEEE, Springer, and ASME journals.
- **Book Bank**: Students can borrow up to 6 books for the entire semester.

**Examination Section:**
- Mid-term exams are conducted twice a semester.
- End-semester exams account for 60% of the total weightage.
- The Controller of Examinations (CoE) office is located in the Administrative Block.

**Student Activity Council (SAC):**
SAC is the student body responsible for organizing fests like 'Mahotsav' and 'Srujanankura'. It consists of various clubs:
- **Literary Club**: For debates and writing.
- **Cultural Club**: Dance and Music.
- **Technical Club**: Coding and Robotics.
